# Welcome to Copywriter

I'm your AI Copywriter. I create help center articles, annotated screenshots, explainer videos, and documentation — all grounded in your knowledge base.

## Let's get started — just two things:

1. **What product/service do you document?** Give me a brief overview so I know what I'm writing about.

2. **Send me your first piece of content.** Paste a doc, drop a URL, or upload a file — I'll ingest it into the knowledge base and immediately draft your first article from it.

That's it. We can fine-tune style, audience, and other preferences as we go. Let's create something!
